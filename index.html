Our Top Three Trending Flavors!

Since 1985, our shop has created the richest, most flavorful ice cream in town. Our milk and cream is freshly sourced from our farms all across the world. Our ingredients are all organic, as we make sure to provide the highest quality experience we can offer. Here are our best selling flavors as of 2022:

Green Tea

Our all time favorite flavor would be the rich and creamy matcha ice cream. Imported from Japan, we use only the highest quality matcha to ensure that you receive the richest flavors that matcha can offer. The earthy tones of this flavor compliment the sweet yet slightly bitter taste that it will offer you.

Mango

Our second best-selling flavor would be our unique mango flavor. We source our mangoes from all over the world, making sure to only select the highest quality there is. This tropical fruit will be sure to refresh you on a hot summer day.

Strawberry

Our third best-selling ice cream would be our classic strawberry. This has been a fan favoite for years. The light ice cream compliments the strawberries found inside the ice cream to create a simple, yet mesmerizing experience.
